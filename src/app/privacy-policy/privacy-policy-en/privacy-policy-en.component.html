If you are in Canada, similar principles apply regarding express or implied consent. You can withdraw your consent by following the instructions below.

In exceptional cases, we may process your information without your consent if necessary for reasons such as fraud prevention, legal investigations, or compliance with a subpoena.

9. CONTROLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems include a Do-Not-Track (“DNT”) feature which you can enable to signal your preference not to have your online browsing activities tracked. At this time, no uniform standard for DNT signals exists and we do not currently respond to such signals.

California law requires us to inform you that we do not respond to DNT signals.
